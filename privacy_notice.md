# Privacy Policy — Curiosity Screen

_Last updated: 2025_

Curiosity Screen respects your privacy.

## Data Collection

Curiosity Screen does **not collect, store, transmit, or share** any personal data.

Specifically:

- No user accounts
- No analytics
- No tracking
- No advertising identifiers
- No personal information of any kind

## Data Usage

All content displayed by Curiosity Screen is processed locally on your Mac.

Any content downloaded by the app remains on the user’s device and is **not sent to any external server**.

## Third-Party Services

Curiosity Screen does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children’s Privacy

Curiosity Screen does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If the app’s behavior changes in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated accordingly.
